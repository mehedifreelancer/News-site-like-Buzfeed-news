Andrew Sipowicz was walking out to his car Monday night near his Buffalo, New York, home when he discovered the front of his red Ford Mustang had been badly damaged. “I was like, 'You gotta be kidding,'" the 21-year-old said. Sipowicz, a senior at Canisius College, told BuzzFeed News that he was "kind of in a panic" after seeing the front of his car crumpled from an apparent hit-and-run. "And that’s when I saw the note," he said.
